PhishGuard Use Cases

Discover how PhishGuard can help protect you and your organization from phishing attacks.

For Individuals

  • Email Link Verification

    Received a suspicious email with a link? Copy and paste it into PhishGuard before clicking to verify its legitimacy.

  • Social Media Protection

    Check shortened URLs often found on social media platforms that might hide malicious destinations.

  • Online Shopping Safety

    Verify the legitimacy of online shopping sites, especially when they offer unusually large discounts.

  • Financial Security

    Double-check banking and payment links to ensure you're not being directed to fraudulent sites.

For Organizations

  • Employee Training

    Use PhishGuard as part of security awareness training to demonstrate how to identify phishing attempts.

  • IT Security Integration

    Integrate PhishGuard's API with your organization's security infrastructure for automated URL checking.

  • Incident Response

    When potential phishing attacks are reported, quickly analyze suspicious URLs to determine their threat level.

  • Vendor Risk Management

    Verify the legitimacy of vendor websites and links before sharing sensitive information or credentials.
