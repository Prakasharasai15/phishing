About PhishGuard Pro

Learn more about our advanced phishing detection system

Our Mission

PhishGuard Pro was created with a simple but crucial mission: to protect internet users from the growing threat of phishing attacks. In an age where digital security is paramount, we aim to provide accessible, accurate, and advanced tools to identify malicious websites.

The Technology

Our system uses a sophisticated machine learning model trained on thousands of legitimate and phishing websites. The model analyzes over 30 different features of a URL and its domain to determine the likelihood of it being a phishing attempt.

Machine Learning

Gradient boosting classifier trained on extensive datasets

Feature Extraction

Comprehensive URL and domain feature analysis

Community Reporting

Crowdsourced threat intelligence from user reports
